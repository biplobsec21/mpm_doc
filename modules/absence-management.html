Absence Management

Track and manage soldier absences

Module Overview

Absence Management handles soldier absences with documented reasons, approval workflows, and audit trails. Submit absence applications, manage approvals, upload supporting documentation, and maintain complete history records.

Key Features

Absence Type Setup

Create absence categories (medical, personal, emergency, etc.) in settings.

Submit Applications

Single or batch absence applications with documented reasons.

Approval Workflow

Pending → Approved/Rejected with documented decisions.

Document Management

Upload supporting documents and manage file attachments.

Search & Filter

Advanced filtering and search across absence records.

History Tracking

Complete audit trail and historical records.

FAQ

Can I upload documents with absence applications?

Yes, attach medical certificates, permission letters, or other supporting documents.

Can I submit absences for multiple soldiers at once?

Yes, batch submission is supported for multiple soldiers.

What happens when an absence is approved?

The soldier becomes unavailable for duty assignment during the absence period.
