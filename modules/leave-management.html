Leave Management

Handle leave requests and approvals

Module Overview

Leave Management streamlines the leave request and approval process. Soldiers submit requests, supervisors review and approve/reject, automatic status management handles soldier availability during leave, and complete history is maintained for auditing.

Key Features

Leave Submission

Soldiers submit leave requests with leave type and dates.

Approval Workflow

Supervisors review and approve/reject leave requests.

Leave Types

Different leave categories managed in settings.

Bulk Approval

Approve multiple leave requests at once.

History Tracking

Complete leave request history and records.

Leave Cancellation

Cancel approved leave if circumstances change.

Business Rules

  • Status Automation: When leave is approved, soldier status automatically changes and becomes unavailable for duty.

  • Duty Conflict Prevention: System prevents duty assignment during approved leave periods.

  • Approval Required: All leave requests must be approved before soldier is marked as on leave.
