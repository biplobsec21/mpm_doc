Soldier Management

Comprehensive personnel record management

Module Overview

Soldier Management is the central repository for all military personnel information. Manage personal details, service history, qualifications, medical records, and status. The system tracks career progression and enables comprehensive personnel searches and bulk operations.

Personnel Records

Complete soldier profiles

Service History

Career progression tracking

Key Features

Create Soldier Profiles

Add new soldiers with essential information including personal data, rank, and company assignment. Profiles can be created individually or in bulk.

Personal Information

Maintain complete personal details - name, date of birth, contact information, next of kin, and government ID numbers. All information is secure and audit-logged.

Service History

Track promotions, transfers, assignments, and career milestones. Create a complete chronological record of service.

Qualifications

Record certifications, training completion, and professional qualifications. Track expiration dates and renewal requirements.

Medical Records

Manage medical fitness, vaccinations, allergies, and health conditions. Maintain HIPAA-compliant secure records.

Status Management

Manage soldier status: Active, On Leave, Medical, Deployed, or Inactive. Status affects duty eligibility automatically.

FAQ

Can I edit soldier information after creation?

Yes, all soldier information can be edited except unique identifiers like ID numbers.

What statuses can a soldier have?

Active, On Leave, Medical, Deployed, Retired, and Inactive. Different statuses affect duty eligibility.

Can I deactivate a soldier?

Yes, set status to Retired or Inactive. This prevents them from being assigned to duties automatically.
