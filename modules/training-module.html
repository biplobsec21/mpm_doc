Training Module

Manage military training and professional development

Module Overview

The Training Module manages three types of training activities: Course assignments, Cadre (instructor) assignments, and Exercise Area participation. Track training status, completion, and create comprehensive professional development records.

Key Features

Course Management

Assign soldiers to courses and track enrollment and completion.

Cadre Assignment

Designate instructors and mentors for training.

Exercise Areas

Manage exercise locations and participation.

Status Management

Track training status automatically (Enrolled, Active, Completed).

Completion Verification

Verify and record training completion with documentation.

Bulk Operations

Efficiently manage multiple training assignments.

Three Types of Training

1. Courses

Formal training programs and certification courses

  • • Duration tracking
  • • Certification
  • • Completion status

2. Cadre

Instructor and mentorship assignments

  • • Instructor tracking
  • • Mentorship roles
  • • Training delivery

3. Exercise Areas

Field exercises and practice locations

  • • Location management
  • • Participation tracking
  • • Exercise scheduling
