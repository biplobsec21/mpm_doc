Appointment Management

Manage soldier appointments and role assignments

Module Overview

Appointment Management handles role assignments and positional responsibilities. Support multiple simultaneous appointments per soldier, manage appointment lifecycles, track current and past appointments, and maintain complete career history.

Key Features

Create Appointments

Define appointment positions and their requirements.

Multiple Simultaneous

Soldiers can hold multiple appointments concurrently.

Current & Past View

View active and historical appointments for each soldier.

Status Management

Automatic status transitions (Active, Released, Completed).

Release Soldiers

Remove soldiers from appointments when duties complete.

Career History

Complete record of all appointments for each soldier.

Business Rules

  • Multiple Roles: Soldiers can hold multiple appointments simultaneously (e.g., Team Lead and Training Officer).

  • Release vs. Completion: Release ends appointment immediately, Completion marks it historically complete.

  • Career History: All appointments form part of soldier's permanent career record.

  • Status Impact: Affects duty assignment eligibility and reporting structures.
